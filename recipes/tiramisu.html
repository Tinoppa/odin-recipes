Tiramisu

Tiramisu a classic Italian no-bake dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and incredible mascarpone cream. The custard-like cream is excellent and contains no raw egg. This easy recipe is truly the best homemade tiramisu and always gets rave reviews.

Ingredients

  • 568ml pot double cream.
  • 250g tub mascarpone.
  • 75ml marsala.
  • 5 tbsp golden caster sugar
  • 300ml strong coffee, made with 2 tbsp coffee granules and 300ml boiling water.
  • 175g pack sponge fingers.
  • 25g dark chocolate.
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder.
